How to Buy

Create Wallet

Install MetaMask, create a new wallet and secure the seed phrase, then transfer ETH to your MetaMask address to buy a token on Ethereum.

Connect to DEX

Connect to a DEX like Uniswap, SushiSwap, or 1inch. Open the DEX website, click "Connect Wallet", and select MetaMask. Approve in MetaMask to trade.

Swap ETH for $OHM

Select ETH in "from" field and your desired token in "to" field. Enter the amount, click "Swap" or "Exchange", and confirm in MetaMask

Manage your $OHM

Check your transaction status in MetaMask under "Activity" or on Etherscan. If the token isn't visible, click "Assets" > "Add Token" and Enter the contract address.
